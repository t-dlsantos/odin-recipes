Coxinha

Description

a quick coxinha recipe for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups wheat
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 chicken stock
  • 1/2 tsp margarine

Steps

  1. Bring the milk, margarine and stock to the boil.
  2. Bring to the boil.
  3. Pour in the wheat all at once and stir well.
  4. Then remove from the pan and place on the table.
  5. Knead a little and start shaping the drumsticks.
  6. Fry in hot oil, wait until golden brown, remove and serve.
