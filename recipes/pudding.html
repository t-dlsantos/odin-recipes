Pudding

Description

A delicious and simple pudding recipe to sweeten your day

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 can of milk
  • 1 carton of cream

Steps

  1. In a pudding pan, melt the sugar, leaving the pan caramelized.
  2. Place the eggs in a blender with the condensed milk, milk and cream.
  3. Then blend for 20 minutes.
  4. Pour into the caramelized mold and place in a preheated oven, in a bain-marie, at 180 degrees Celsius.
